  • New School Canteen

    A school canteen is a facility that provides meals to students and can also be a place to promote nutrition and sustainability. Outside of the U.S. and Canada, a cafeteria is sometimes called a canteen. Here are some things to consider about school food canteens: Size The standard length of a school canteen is 160â€“180 cm, and the standard size per child is 60 cm. Food Some healthy snack options include fresh fruit, carrot sticks, and low-fat milk. Some popular drinks include water, juice, milk, and soda. Lunch items can include sandwiches, wraps, salads, and hot foods like pizza or pasta. Promotions To boost canteen sales, you can keep the food service area, food display areas, and equipment clean and tidy. You can also create a welcoming environment by keeping the area and furniture around the canteen clean, tidy, and inviting. You can also ask classes or student groups to help decorate the canteen area with art works, posters, and signs. Convenience School lunches are often served in a cafeteria setting, which can be a convenient way for children to get a healthy meal during the school day. This is particularly important for children who may not have access to healthy food options at home.
